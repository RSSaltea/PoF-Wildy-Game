Crafting

Commands for crafting items, breaking them down, runecrafting, gem cutting, enchanting, and consuming.

!w craftables

View all craftable items and their recipes.

!w craft <item>

Craft an item if you have the required materials.

!w breakdownitems

View all items that can be broken down.

!w breakdown <item>

Break down an item into its components.

!w rc <rune>

Aliases: !w runecraft

Runecraft runes using pure essence and pouches.

!w cut [qty] <gem>

Cut uncut gems from your bank into cut gems. Each gem takes 1.2 seconds to cut. You can specify a quantity (!w cut 5 sapphire) or cut all (!w cut all sapphire). Cannot be done in the Wilderness.

!w cut stop

Stop cutting gems early. Any gems already cut are added to your bank and remaining uncut gems are returned.

!w enchant <item>

Enchant an item using runes from your bank. For example, !w enchant omnigem amulet creates the Eclipse of the Five. Run !w enchant with no arguments to see all recipes.

!w consume <item>

Consume an item for its effect. For example, !w consume ancient effigy grants Slayer XP based on your Slayer level. See the Ancient Effigy page for the full XP table. Run !w consume with no arguments to see all consumables.

!w consume auto [item]

Toggle auto-consume for an item. When toggled on, that item will automatically be consumed for XP whenever you receive it as a drop. View your auto-consume list with !w consume auto (no item).
