Crafting

Commands for crafting items, breaking them down, and runecrafting.

!w craftables

View all craftable items and their recipes.

!w craft <item>

Craft an item if you have the required materials.

!w breakdownitems

View all items that can be broken down.

!w breakdown <item>

Break down an item into its components.

!w rc <rune>

Aliases: !w runecraft

Runecraft runes using pure essence and pouches.
