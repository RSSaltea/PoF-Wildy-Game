Inventory Management

Commands for viewing, equipping, dropping, and managing items in your inventory.

!w inv

Aliases: !w inventory

View your inventory.

!w equip <item>

Equip an item to its slot. Mainhand/offhand must have matching combat styles. Items can be equipped from inventory or bank (bank only when not in wilderness).

!w unequip <slot>

Remove an equipped item. Use !w unequip all to unequip everything.

!w gear

Aliases: !w worn

View your currently equipped gear.

!w drop <item> [qty]

Drop items from your inventory.

!w inspect <item>

Aliases: !w insp

View detailed item stats and effects.

!w examine [@player]

Aliases: !w look, !w inspectplayer

View another player's equipped gear.

!w ground

View items on the ground from your current wilderness run.

!w ground pickup <item> [qty]

Aliases: !w ground pick

Pick up items from the ground.

!w blacklist [item]

View your blacklist or add an item. Blacklisted items are auto-dropped on loot.

!w blacklist remove <item>

Remove an item from your blacklist.

!w blacklist clear

Clear your entire blacklist.

!w lock <item>

Lock an item so it stays in inventory during deposit all.

!w lock remove <item>

Remove a lock.
