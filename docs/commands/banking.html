Banking

Commands for storing and retrieving items from your bank.

!w bank

Aliases: !w bankview

View your bank contents. Uses a dropdown menu to switch between categories.

!w deposit

Aliases: !w depo

Deposit all inventory items into your bank. Respects locked items (they stay in inventory). Noted items are automatically converted to unnoted. Use !w deposit all to also deposit coins.

!w depo <item>

Deposit all of a specific item from your inventory into your bank. This overrides deposit locks, so locked items can be deposited by name. Example: !w depo manta

!w withdraw [qty] <item> [noted]

Withdraw items from your bank. Add noted at the end to withdraw as noted.
