Miscellaneous

Commands for viewing stats, highscores, pets, NPCs, and other features.

!w stats

Aliases: !w profile, !w me

View your full player profile: kills, deaths, escapes, coins, slayer info, and more.

!w kc [npc]

Aliases: !w killcount

View your kill count for a specific NPC or all NPCs.

!w highscores [category]

Aliases: !w hs

View the highscores with an interactive dropdown. Categories: Overall, Kills, Deaths, Wealth, Slayer, Unique Drops, Pets, Tasks.

!w npcs

Browse all NPCs with an interactive page selector showing stats, slayer info, and drop rates.

!w pets

Aliases: !w pet

View your full pet collection. Shows all pets in the game — owned pets display normally with a duplicate count if received more than once, unowned pets appear with strikethrough.

!w pets <name>

Look up a specific pet to see where it drops from and whether you own it (including how many times you've received it).

!w chest

View available chests to open.

!w chest open mysterious

Open a chest using a Mysterious key. Rewards a random selection of loot.

!w chest open bone

Open a chest using a Bone key. Rewards a random selection of loot.
