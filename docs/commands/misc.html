Miscellaneous

Commands for viewing stats, highscores, pets, NPCs, and other features.

!w stats

Aliases: !w profile, !w me

View your full player profile: kills, deaths, escapes, coins, slayer info, and more.

!w kc [npc]

Aliases: !w killcount

View your kill count for a specific NPC or all NPCs.

!w highscores [category]

Aliases: !w hs

View the highscores with an interactive dropdown. Categories: Overall, Kills, Deaths, Wealth, Slayer, Unique Drops, Pets, Tasks.

!w npcs

Browse all NPCs with an interactive page selector showing stats, slayer info, and drop rates.

!w pets

View your pet collection.

!w pet <name>

Look up a specific pet.

!w chest

View available chests to open.

!w chest open <type>

Open a chest using keys.
