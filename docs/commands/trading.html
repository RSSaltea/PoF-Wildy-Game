Trading

Commands for trading items with other players.

!w trade @player

Send a trade request to another player.

!w trade accept

Accept an incoming trade request.

!w trade add [qty] <item>

Add items to the trade window.

!w trade remove <item> [qty]

Remove items from the trade window.

!w trade cancel

Cancel the current trade.
