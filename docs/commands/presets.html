Presets

Commands for saving and loading inventory and equipment presets.

!w presets

List your saved inventory presets.

!w preset create <name>

Save your current inventory and equipment as a preset.

!w preset override <name>

Overwrite an existing preset with your current inventory.

!w preset load <name>

Load a preset, withdrawing items from your bank.

!w preset delete <name>

Delete a saved preset.

!w preset check <name>

Check if your bank has the items needed for a preset.
