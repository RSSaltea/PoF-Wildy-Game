Getting Started

New to the Wilderness? Follow these steps to get up and running.

1. Create Your Profile

!w start

Create your wilderness profile. You'll receive a Starter Sword (+2 ATK) and Starter Platebody (+2 DEF) along with some starting coins and full HP.

2. Equip Your Gear

Before you can fight, you need to equip your weapons and armour.

!w equip <item>

Equip an item from your inventory to the appropriate slot.

  • !w equip starter sword
  • !w equip starter platebody

!w gear

Aliases: worn

View your currently equipped items.

3. Venture Into the Wilderness

Use !w venture to enter the Wilderness. Start low — Wilderness level 1–5 is safest for beginners. Higher levels unlock stronger NPCs with better drops, but the danger increases too.

!w venture <level>

Enter or move to a wilderness level. Example: !w venture 5

4. Fight NPCs

Once you're in the Wilderness, battle creatures to earn coins, loot, and slayer XP. Start with the Revenant Goblin (Tier 1, Wildy 1+).

!w fight <npc>

Fight an NPC at your current wilderness level. Example: !w fight revenant goblin

5. Stay Alive

Keep an eye on your health and bank your loot often. If you die, you lose items in your inventory!

!w eat [qty] [food]

Eat food to restore HP. Buy food from the !w shop.

!w hp

Aliases: health

Check your current health.

!w tele

Aliases: teleport, tp

Teleport out of the Wilderness to safety.

!w deposit

Deposit your inventory to the bank. Use !w deposit all to deposit everything including coins.

6. Progress Further

  • Slayer — Use !w slayer task to get assignments for bonus XP and points. See the Slayer Guide.
  • Crafting — Combine drops into powerful gear. Check !w craftables for recipes.
  • Runecrafting — Craft runes from Pure essence. Runes fuel equipment effects and enchanting.
  • Gem Cutting — Cut uncut gems into cut gems, then combine them into an Omnigem.
  • Enchanting — Create powerful items like the Eclipse of the Five.
  • Shopping — Buy better weapons, armour, and food from the shop.
  • Alchemy — Convert drops to coins or set up auto-alch.
  • Trading — Trade items with other players.
  • Presets — Save and load gear loadouts quickly.

Crafting & Skills

!w craft <item>

Craft an item from materials in your bank. Use !w craftables to see all recipes.

!w rc <rune>

Aliases: runecraft

Craft runes from Pure essence in your bank. Pouches in your inventory increase the amount crafted. You'll be busy for 25 seconds while crafting.

!w cut <gem>

Cut uncut gems from your bank into cut gems. Each gem takes 1.2 seconds to cut. You can specify a quantity (!w cut 5 sapphire) or cut all (!w cut all sapphire). Use !w cut stop to stop early. Cannot be done in the Wilderness.

!w enchant <item>

Enchant an item using runes from your bank. For example, !w enchant omnigem amulet creates the Eclipse of the Five (requires 10 Cosmic runes and 5 Bone Runes).

!w consume <item>

Consume an item for its effect. For example, !w consume ancient effigy grants Slayer XP based on your Slayer level. See the Ancient Effigy page for the full XP table. Use !w consume auto <item> to auto-consume drops.

Other Useful Commands

!w trade <@player>

Start a trade with another player. Use !w trade add, !w trade remove, !w trade accept, and !w trade cancel to manage trades.

!w preset create <name>

Save your current equipped gear as a preset. Load it later with !w preset load <name>. Use !w presets to see your saved presets.

!w drink

Drink a potion from your inventory for a temporary combat buff. Strength potions grant +2 ATK for 10 hits, Super Strength potions grant +4 ATK for 20 hits.

!w alch <item>

Convert items to coins using Nature runes. Use !w alch auto <item> to auto-alch that item whenever you receive it.

As you grow stronger, venture deeper into the Wilderness to face higher-tier NPCs and bosses with powerful unique drops and pets.

Reset Your Profile

!w reset

Wipe your profile and start fresh. This cannot be undone.
