Getting Started

New to the Wilderness? You only need 6 commands to start playing.

Tip: Type !w quickstart in Discord for this same guide as an interactive embed, or !w help to see every command.

The Gameplay Loop

Start → Equip → Venture → Fight → Bank → Repeat

That's it. Everything else — slayer, crafting, trading — you'll discover as you play. The game gives you tips along the way.

1. Create Your Profile

!w start

Creates your profile. You'll get a Starter Sword, Starter Platebody, some coins, and full HP.

2. Equip Your Gear

!w equip starter sword

!w equip starter platebody

You need gear equipped to deal damage and take less. Use !w gear to see what you're wearing.

3. Buy Some Food

!w shop buy 10 lobster

Food heals you automatically during combat when your HP gets low. You can also eat manually with !w eat.

4. Enter the Wilderness

!w venture 5

Start at a low level (1–5). Higher wilderness levels unlock stronger NPCs with better loot.

5. Fight!

!w fight

Fights a random NPC at your wilderness level. You can also target one: !w fight revenant goblin. Start with the Revenant Goblin (Tier 1).

6. Bank Your Loot

!w tele

Teleport out of the Wilderness to safety.

!w deposit

Alias: depo

Bank your inventory. If you die, you lose everything you're carrying — so bank often!

What's Next?

Once you're comfortable with the basics, explore these systems at your own pace:

  • Slayer — !w slayer task gives you assignments for bonus XP and points towards powerful rewards.
  • Shop — !w shop list to buy better weapons, armour, and food.
  • Crafting — !w craftables to see what you can make from your drops.
  • Grand Exchange — !w ge to buy and sell items with other players.
  • Alchemy — !w alch to convert items to coins.
  • Trading — !w trade @player to trade directly with someone.
  • Presets — !w preset create to save gear loadouts for quick switching.
  • Combat Settings — !w autoeat and !w warning to customise your combat experience.

As you grow stronger, venture deeper into the Wilderness to face higher-tier NPCs and bosses with powerful unique drops and pets.

Reset Your Profile

!w reset

Wipe your profile and start fresh. This cannot be undone.
