Combat

Commands for fighting NPCs, attacking players, and surviving the Wilderness.

!w venture [level]

Enter the Wilderness at a specific level (1–50), or a random level if none specified. You can venture deeper or back to lower levels. Going deeper has a chance to skull you.

!w fight [npc]

Fight an NPC. If you specify an NPC name, there's a 75% chance you'll encounter it (100% with Bracelet of Slayer Aggression on task). Otherwise you get a random encounter.

!w attack @player

Attack another player in the Wilderness for a PvP duel.

!w tele

Aliases: !w teleport, !w tp

Attempt to teleport out. 80% success rate; 20% chance of an ambush.

!w eat [qty] [food]

Eat food to restore HP. If no food specified, eats your best food.

!w drink [potion]

Drink a potion for temporary stat boosts.

!w hp

Aliases: !w health

Check your current HP.
