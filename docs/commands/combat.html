Combat

Commands for fighting NPCs, attacking players, and surviving the Wilderness.

!w venture [level]

Enter the Wilderness at a specific level (1–50), or a random level if none specified. You can venture deeper or back to lower levels. Going deeper has a chance to skull you.

!w fight [npc]

Fight an NPC. If you specify an NPC name, there's a 75% chance you'll encounter it (100% with Bracelet of Slayer Aggression on task). Otherwise you get a random encounter.

!w attack @player

Attack another player in the Wilderness for a PvP duel.

!w tele

Aliases: !w teleport, !w tp

Attempt to teleport out. 80% success rate; 20% chance of an ambush.

!w eat [qty] [food]

Eat food to restore HP. If no food specified, eats your best food.

!w drink [potion]

Drink a potion for temporary stat boosts.

!w hp

Aliases: !w health

Check your current HP.

Combat Settings

!w autoeat <hp>

Set the HP threshold for auto-eating during combat. When your HP drops to this value or below, you'll automatically eat your best food. Use !w autoeat 0 to reset to default. Run with no argument to view your current setting.

!w warning

View your current warning settings. Warnings appear on the fight summary to alert you of dangerous situations.

!w warning health <hp>

Set a health warning threshold. After a fight, if your HP ended below this value, a warning will appear on the fight summary. Use !w warning health 0 to disable.

!w warning food <amount>

Set a food warning threshold. After a fight, if your remaining food count is at or below this amount, a warning will appear on the fight summary. Use !w warning food 0 to disable.
