Ancient Effigy

Notes

A rare and mysterious artefact pulsing with ancient energy. Can be consumed with !w consume Ancient Effigy to grant Slayer XP based on your current Slayer level, following the Medium XP lamp table. Use !w consume auto Ancient Effigy to automatically consume it whenever it drops. Can also be sold for coins or used with High Alchemy.
