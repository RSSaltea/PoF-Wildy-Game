Abyssal Overlord

The Abyssal Overlord is a colossal entity from the depths of the Abyss, commanding legions of lesser abyssal creatures. Its massive frame belies a cunning intelligence, and the treasures it hoards are among the most valuable in the Wilderness. Slayers brave enough to challenge the Overlord may find themselves rewarded with the legendary Abyssal Scourge.
