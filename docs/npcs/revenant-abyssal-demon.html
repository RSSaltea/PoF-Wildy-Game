Revenant Abyssal Demon

Torn from the Abyss and bound to the Wilderness as a restless spirit, the Revenant Abyssal Demon is a fearsome hybrid of demonic rage and spectral energy. It prowls the deep wilderness, hoarding pure essence and the occasional Abyssal Whip — a prized weapon among adventurers.
