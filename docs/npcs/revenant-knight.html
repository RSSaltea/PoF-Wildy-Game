Revenant Knight

Once a noble warrior sworn to protect the realm, the Revenant Knight now patrols the mid-level Wilderness as a hollow suit of spectral armour. Its strikes are precise and its defences formidable for its tier. Many adventurers underestimate the Knight, mistaking it for easy prey — a mistake they rarely make twice.
