Revenant Necromancer

The Revenant Necromancer is a glass cannon among the spectral hordes. In life, it was a practitioner of forbidden death magic, and its power has only grown in undeath. With devastating attack power but virtually no defences, the Necromancer rewards aggressive fighters who can bring it down quickly. It is the only source of the Death Guard and Skull Lantern — necromantic weapons of terrifying power.
