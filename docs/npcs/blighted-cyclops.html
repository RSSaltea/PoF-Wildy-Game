Blighted Cyclops

A towering one-eyed beast corrupted by the blight that seeps through the deep Wilderness. The Blighted Cyclops guards a hoard of defenders — shields forged from the bones and metal of fallen warriors. Slayers seeking to upgrade their offhand equipment will find themselves returning to this creature again and again.
