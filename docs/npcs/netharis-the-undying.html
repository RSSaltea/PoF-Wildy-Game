Netharis the Undying

Netharis the Undying is a fearsome creature lurking in the Wilderness — an ancient lich who has cheated death so many times that death itself has given up trying. With 550 HP, 18 attack, and 14 defence, Netharis is a gruelling endurance fight. Its Curse ability steadily strips away your defences, making each passing turn more dangerous than the last. Well-prepared slayers with solid gear and plenty of food should attempt this fight.

Abilities

Curse — 15% chance per turn

Curses the player, reducing their defence by 4 for the next 5 hits.
