Netharis the Undying

Netharis the Undying is the most powerful boss in the Wilderness. This ancient necromantic entity commands overwhelming dark magic and is nearly impossible to kill. Only the strongest and most well-equipped adventurers dare face her.

Special Ability

Undying Grasp (12%) — Heals Netharis and reduces player's defence
