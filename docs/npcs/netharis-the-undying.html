Netharis the Undying

Netharis the Undying is the most powerful creature in the Wilderness — an ancient lich who has cheated death so many times that death itself has given up trying. With 550 HP, 18 attack, and 14 defence, Netharis is a gruelling endurance fight. Its Curse ability steadily strips away your defences, making each passing turn more dangerous than the last. Only the strongest slayers, armed with the best gear and plenty of food, should attempt this fight.

Abilities

Curse — 15% chance per turn

Curses the player, reducing their defence by 4 for the next 5 hits.
