Masked Figure

No one knows who — or what — lurks behind the Masked Figure's featureless facade. It appears without warning in the deepest reaches of the Wilderness, moving with an unnatural grace that suggests something far more dangerous than a mere mortal. The Masked Figure is the sole guardian of the Black Mask, a crucial component in crafting the Slayer Helmet.
