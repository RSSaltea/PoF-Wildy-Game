Lord Valthyros

Lord Valthyros is an ancient blood mage who has transcended mortality through forbidden rituals. With the highest defence of any NPC in its tier, Valthyros is a war of attrition — slowly draining your resources while shrugging off your attacks. Those who persevere may earn the Ring of Valthyros or the coveted Amulet of Seeping.
