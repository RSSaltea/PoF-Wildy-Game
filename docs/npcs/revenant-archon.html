Revenant Archon

The Revenant Archon is the most powerful of the spectral revenants, a once-mighty general who commanded armies in life. In death, its authority has been replaced with raw, unbridled fury. Found deep in the Wilderness, the Archon is sought after for its generous ether drops and the chance at powerful revenant relics.
