Chaos Fanatic

A deranged mage who has embraced the raw chaos of the Wilderness. The Chaos Fanatic hurls unpredictable spells and mutters incantations that make no sense to mortal ears. Despite its madness, it guards a trove of chaos runes and rare treasures, including the coveted Bracelet of Slayer Aggression.
