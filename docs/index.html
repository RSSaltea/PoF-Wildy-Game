Getting Started

New to the Wilderness? Follow these steps to get up and running.

1. Create Your Profile

Type !w start in a Discord channel where the bot is active. You'll receive a Starter Sword (+2 ATK) and Starter Platebody (+2 DEF) along with some starting coins and full HP.

2. Equip Your Gear

Before you can fight, you need to equip your weapons and armour:

  • !w equip starter sword
  • !w equip starter platebody
  • Use !w gear to check what you have equipped

3. Venture Into the Wilderness

Use !w venture <level> to enter the Wilderness. Start low — Wilderness level 1–5 is safest for beginners. Higher levels unlock stronger NPCs with better drops, but the danger increases too.

4. Fight NPCs

Once you're in the Wilderness, use !w fight <npc> to battle creatures. Start with the Revenant Goblin (Tier 1, Wildy 1+). Each kill earns you coins, loot, and slayer XP if you have a task assigned.

5. Stay Alive

  • !w eat — Eat food to restore HP (buy food from the !w shop)
  • !w hp — Check your current health
  • !w tele — Teleport out of the Wilderness to safety

If you die, you lose items in your inventory — so bank often with !w deposit!

6. Progress Further

  • Slayer — Use !w slayer task to get assignments for bonus XP and points. See the Slayer Guide.
  • Crafting — Combine drops into better gear with !w craft. Check !w craftables for recipes.
  • Runecrafting — Craft runes from Pure essence with !w rc <rune>. Runes fuel equipment effects and enchanting.
  • Gem Cutting — Cut uncut gems from your bank with !w cut <gem>. Cut gems are used to craft the Omnigem.
  • Enchanting — Enchant items with !w enchant <item>. Create powerful gear like the Eclipse of the Five.
  • Consuming — Use consumable items with !w consume <item>. For example, consume an Ancient Effigy for Slayer XP.
  • Shopping — Buy better weapons, armour, and food from !w shop list.
  • Banking — Keep your valuables safe with !w deposit and !w bank.
  • Alchemy — Convert drops to coins with !w alch or set up auto-alch with !w alch auto.
  • Trading — Trade items with other players using !w trade @player.
  • Presets — Save and load gear loadouts with !w preset create <name> and !w preset load <name>.
  • Potions — Drink combat potions for temporary buffs with !w drink.

As you grow stronger, venture deeper into the Wilderness to face higher-tier NPCs and bosses with powerful unique drops and pets.
