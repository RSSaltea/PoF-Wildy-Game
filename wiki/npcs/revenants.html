Revenants

Restless spirits that haunt the Wilderness, bound to the mortal plane by unfinished business and spectral rage.

Revenants are the spectral remains of warriors, mages, and creatures that perished in the Wilderness. They roam freely across all wilderness levels and share a common drop table of Revenant ether, Revenant Relic Shards, Revenant Totems, and the coveted Viggora's Chainmace. All revenants can also drop the Bracelet of ethereum and have a chance at the Tiny Revenant pet.

The Bracelet of ethereum provides 50% damage reduction against all Revenant-type NPCs when worn, making it essential for extended farming sessions.
