Zarveth the Veilbreaker

Zarveth the Veilbreaker is an eldritch horror that exists between dimensions, tearing holes in reality itself. With the highest base attack of any NPC, Zarveth hits like a truck but has surprisingly low defences — a trade-off that rewards aggressive, well-geared fighters. Its signature Veil Shatter ability can turn the tide of battle in an instant. Zarveth drops the powerful Veilbreaker weapon and the Ascendant armour set.

Abilities

Veil Shatter — 5% chance per turn

Shatters the veil, forcing the player's next hit to deal 0 damage.
