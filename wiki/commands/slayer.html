Slayer System

Commands for the slayer skill, including tasks, the slayer shop, and progression tracking.

!w slayer

View your slayer info: level, XP, points, current task progress.

!w slayer task

Get a new slayer task or view your current one. Tasks are weighted by slayer level — higher level NPCs are more likely to be assigned.

!w slayer skip

Skip your current task for 30 slayer points.

!w slayer shop

View the slayer shop. Currently sells: Slayer Helmet Unlock (2,500 points).

!w slayer buy <item>

Purchase from the slayer shop.

!w slayer npcs

List all slayer NPCs with their required level and XP per kill.

!w slayer block [npc]

View your block list or block an NPC from task assignments (500 points, max 3 blocks).

!w slayer block remove <npc>

Remove an NPC from your block list (no refund).

Note: Slayer XP is only earned while on task. Point rewards scale with task streaks: 5 per task, 20 every 10th, 80 every 40th, 140 every 100th, 280 every 500th.
