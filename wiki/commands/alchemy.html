Alchemy

Commands for converting items into coins through high alchemy.

!w alch <item>

High-alch items for coins. Costs 1 Nature rune per item. Alchs your entire stack. Supports aliases (e.g. !w alch rune scim).

!w alch auto [item]

Toggle auto-alch for an item. When toggled on, that item will automatically be alched when received as a combat drop (including noted items). View your auto-alch list with !w alch auto (no item).
